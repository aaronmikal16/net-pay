Net Pay Program

V.1

will calculate the net pay of a broke boy using gross pay and deducting federal, state, and fica. it has options like deducting additional ammounts from your check. then it displays your net pay.

  1. Start
  2. set variables fed=.15 state=.05 fica=.0762
  3. Display "enter gross pay"
  4. input gross_pay
  5. Display "any additional deductions"
  6. input additional
  7. tax1=gross_pay*fed
  8. tax2=gross_pay*state
  9. tax3=gross_pay*fica
  10. taxes=tax1+tax2+tax3+additional
  11. net_pay=gross_pay-taxes
  12. Display "your net pay after is net_pay"
  13. End
